Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

You can cancel your booking up to 4 hours before the showtime from the 'My Orders' section. Cancellation charges may apply.

Where is my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and credited back to the original payment method.

Do I need to print my ticket?

No! BookMyTicket is completely paperless. Just show the QR code on your app at the cinema entrance.
